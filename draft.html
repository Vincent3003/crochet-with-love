Who Are We?

Welcome to Adorable.doo, where every stitch is crafted with care and creativity! We take pride in offering 100% handmade crochet items, lovingly made with the finest materials and attention to detail. Our products are designed to be safe for children to use and play with, giving parents peace of mind while their little ones enjoy our soft and durable creations. Whether you're looking for unique gifts or cozy accessories, each piece is crafted with love and passion. Plus, crochet has the added benefit of helping people stay focused and mindful—perfect for enhancing your everyday moments!


In addition to creating beautiful, one-of-a-kind pieces, we believe in giving back—part of our profits are donated to support local orphanages, helping children in need. With every purchase, you’re not only getting a product made with love but also making a positive impact in the lives of others! By shopping with us, you’re not only receiving a product made with love but also supporting a small business with a big heart. Thank you for your purchase and for being a part of this journey with us!

Long Neck Duck

Materials:

  • Yarns 3.5mm (white, yellow, other your favorite colors)
  • 3.5mm crochet hook, scissor

Stitches + Abbreviations:

  • Ch = chain
  • sc = single crochet
  • st = stitch
  • sl st = slip stitch
  • inc = increase
  • dec = decrease

    • Step 1:

    Head, Neck & Body:
    • R1: Magic Ring 6
    • R2: 6v (12)
    • R3: 1st - 1v (18st)
    • R4: 18st
    • R5: 2st - 1v (24st)
    • R6-7: 24st
    • R8: 2st - 1v (18st)
    • R9: 1st - 1a (12st)
    • R10: 2st - 1a (9st)
    • R11 - 34: 9x
    • R35: 2st - 1a (12st)
    • R36: 12st
    • R37: 1st - 1v (18st)
    • R38: 2st - 1v (24st)
    • R39: 24st
    • R40: 5st - 1v (28st)
    • R41: 6st - 1v (32st)
    • R42: 32st
    • R43: 6st - 1a
    • R44: 5st - 1a (24st)
    • R45: 2st - 1a (18st)
    • R46: 1st - 1a (19st)
    • R47: 6a

    Step 2:

    Wings:
  • 5ch - 1slst - 2st - 3st
  • Do similar with the other side
  • 2st - 1slst

    • Step 3:

    Legs:
  • 8ch - 2st - 5slst

    • Step 4:

    Scarf
  • 25ch - 24st

    • Step 5:

    Sewing:
  • Sewing 2 legs under the body
  • Sewing scarf to the end of the neck
  • Glue eye in the head
